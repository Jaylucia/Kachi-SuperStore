# Kachi Superstore Business Intelligence Analysis

## Project Objective
To analyze sales and profitability data from Kachi Superstore and provide data-driven business recommendations.

## Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Power BI

## Key Business Questions
- Are sales growing or declining?
- Which products and categories drive revenue?
- How do discounts affect profit?
- Which regions are underperforming?
- How efficient are shipping methods?
- Who are the most valuable customers?

## Key Findings
- Strong sales but weak profitability (≈12% profit margin).
- Higher discounts lead to lower profit and negative margins.
- Central region performs well in sales but poorly in profit.
- Several products consistently lose money.
- Second Class shipping is most profitable.
- Repeat customer rate is high (~98%).

## Recommendations
- Reduce excessive discounting.
- Remove or reprice loss-making products.
- Optimize shipping strategy.
- Focus on profit rather than sales.
- Improve Central region product mix.
